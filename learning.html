The Compounding Advantage — Your Career Moat
Your unique edge isn't any single track — it's the intersection. LLM + Observability is nearly empty territory. An AI engineer who can instrument and monitor LLM systems in production is extremely rare. By Month 5, you'll have the only portfolio in most rooms that combines agentic AI + OTel + cloud deployment + a vendor cert.
→Most AI engineers can't monitor their own systems. They rely on basic logging and pray. You'll know p95 latency, cost per call, and drift detection — in production.
→Most observability engineers haven't touched LLMs. They're stuck monitoring Java heaps. You'll bridge both worlds and speak both languages.
→Post about that intersection every chance you get. One well-written post about "LLM Observability in Production" reaches engineers who have never seen that perspective. That's your brand moat.
→The compounding starts now, not in Month 6. Every post, every commit, every instrumented project adds a layer. Don't wait to be "ready" — ready is a myth.
